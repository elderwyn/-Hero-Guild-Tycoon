 #### Methods of Recruitment
---
| Method | Description | Unlock Condition | Cost / Requirements |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Basic Recruitment** | Hire common units for your empire | Available from start | Gold or in-game currency |
| **Advanced Recruitment** | Unlock stronger units | Reach certain guild level or prestige | Gold + Gems / Gamepass option |
| **Event Recruitment** | Limited-time units with unique abilities | Active seasonal events | Event currency / Robux |
| **Hero Summon** | Random chance to get rare units | Can unlock after prestige | Premium currency (Robux) |

#### Recruitment Rules
---
- Players can **hire multiples of the same unit**, but higher-tier units are more expensive.  
- Units are automatically leveled slightly based on guild level or hero upgrades.  
- Certain rare units are **locked until the empire reaches a milestone**.
